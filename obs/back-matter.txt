==== Gayyata! ====

Muna so mu watsa wannan ƙarami Littafi Mai Tsarki cikin //kowane harshe cikin duniya // kuma kuna iya taimakonmu!  Aikin mai yiyuwa ne--muna tunnani zai faru idan dukan jikin Kiristi yana aiki tare don fasara da rarraba albarkun.
==== Rabawa hannu sake ====

Raba wannan albarkun kamar yadda ku ke so ba fasawa.  Duka na cikin na'ura kyautai ne bisa internet, kuma domin lisans abuɗe take don amfani, kuna iya sake buga Labarun Littafi Mai Tsarki (Open Bible Stories) don saidawa ko'ina cikin duniya ba za ku biya kuɗin kamasho.\\
Samu ƙarin bayani a  [[http://openbiblestories.com|http://openbiblestories.com]].

==== Ƙarawa! ====

Samu Labarun Littafin Mai Tsarki (Open Bible Stories) kamar funafune da aɓli selula cikin waɗansu harsuna a [[http://openbiblestories.com|http://openbiblestories.com]].  Bisa hanyoyi saduwa da na'ura kuna iya samu taimako fasarar Labarun Littafin Mai Tsarki cikin harshenku.
