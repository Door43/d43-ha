====== 34. Yesu yana koyaswa ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu ya faɗi wasu labari da dama akan mulkin Allah. Kwatane, ya ce :" Mulkin Allah yana kama da ƙwayar mastard da aka shubka a cikin gona. Kun sani da cewa ƙwayar mastard ita ce mafi ƙanƙanuwa a cikin dukan iri".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-02.jpg?direct&}}

" Amma in ƙwayar mastar ta tsiro, tana zama ice mafi girma cikin dukan shuka garka, da girma sosai har da tsuntsaye suna sabka suna hutawa a bisan reshennu ta".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu ya faɗi wani labari:" Mulkin Allah yana kama da yesty da wata mace take damawa cikin garin brodi, yana kumbura dukan ƙullin".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-04.jpg?direct&}}

" Mulkin Allah yana kuwa kama da ajiyar da wani ya ɓoye a cikin gona. Wani mutum ya samu ajiyar, sai ya bizne ta hallo. Ya cika da farin ciki har ya je ya saida dukan mallakarsa, kuma ya yi anfani da ƙuɗin ya sayi gonar".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-05.jpg?direct&}}

" Mulkin Allah kuma yana kama da ingantacen lu'u lu'u. Da wani mai kasuwancin lu'u-lu'u ya same shi, sai ya saida dukan mallakarsa ya yi anfani da kuɗin ya saye ta".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-06.jpg?direct&}}

Sai Yesu ya faɗawa wasu mutane da suke dogara ga kyaukawan aikinsu, kuma suna ƙyamar waɗansu, wani labari. Ya ce :" Mutum biyu suka tafi Haikali garin yin addu'a. Ɗaya daga cikinsu mai ƙarbar haraji ne, kuma ɗayan shugaban addini ne".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-07.jpg?direct&}}

" Shugaban addinin ya yi addu'a kamar haka:" Na gode Allah da shi ke ni ba mai zunubi bane kamar sauran maza, ko kamar ɓarayi, marasa adilci, fasiƙai, ko kuwa kamar wannan mai ƙarɓar haraji".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-08.jpg?direct&}}

"Bayani, ina azumi sau biyu kowane sati, kuma ina baka zakkar dukan kuɗi da kayan da nake samu".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-09.jpg?direct&}}

" Amma mai ƙabar haraji ya tsaya nesa da shugaban addinin, kuma bai ma ɗaga kai sama ba. A maimakon haka ya buga ƙirjinsa da dunƙullen hannu, kuma ya yi addu'a kamar haka:" Ka ji ƙai na ya Allah, gama ni mai zunubi ne".

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-10.jpg?direct&}}

Sai Yesu ya ce:" Ina gaya maka gaskiya, Allah ya ji addu'ar mai ƙarɓar haraji, kuma ya shaida shi mai addini ne. Amma bai ƙarɓi addu'ar shugaban addini ba. Allah zai ƙasƙantar da duk wanda yake ɗaga kansa, kuma ya ɗaukaka duka wanda ya ƙasƙantar da kan shi".

//Labarin LMT daga Matiyu 13:31-33, 44-46; Marƙus 4:30-32; Luka 13:18-21; 18:9-14//
